About Burton And Associates

Excellence in Legal Representation Since Our Founding

Our Story

Burton And Associates was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide exceptional legal representation while maintaining the highest standards of integrity and professionalism. Since our establishment, we have grown into one of the world's most respected international law firms.

Under the leadership of founder Charles Burton and managing partner Jim Goodman, our firm has built a reputation for achieving favorable outcomes for our clients across a wide range of practice areas. We combine decades of legal experience with a personal approach that ensures every client receives the attention and representation they deserve.

Our Mission

To provide our clients with superior legal services through experienced advocacy, strategic thinking, and unwavering dedication to achieving the best possible outcomes across multiple jurisdictions.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We conduct our practice with the highest ethical standards
  • Excellence: We strive for superior results in every case
  • Dedication: We are committed to our clients' success
  • Experience: We leverage our extensive legal knowledge
  • Global Reach: We provide seamless international legal services

Global Legal Excellence

Burton And Associates operates as an international law firm with a truly global reach. Our headquarters are located in London, United Kingdom, with our regional office serving clients throughout Maryland and the greater United States.
